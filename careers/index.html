Facility Manager

Ilesa, Osun State Full-time On-site

We are looking for an experienced and proactive Facility Manager to oversee the day-to-day operations of the Eden 54 complex — including our luxury apartments, restaurant & lounge, pool area, game house, and barbing salon. You will ensure the facility runs smoothly, safely, and to the highest standard of luxury hospitality.

Responsibilities

  • Oversee maintenance and upkeep of all facilities
  • Manage vendors, contractors, and service providers
  • Ensure health, safety, and cleanliness standards are met
  • Coordinate with department heads on daily operations
  • Handle guest complaints and escalations promptly
  • Monitor budgets and control operational costs

Requirements

  • 3+ years experience in facility or operations management
  • Experience in hospitality or luxury property preferred
  • Strong leadership and communication skills
  • Ability to multitask and work under pressure
  • HND or B.Sc in Facility Management, Estate Management, or related field
  • Proficient in MS Office and reporting tools
