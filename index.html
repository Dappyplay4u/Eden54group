Day Trips & Experiences

Explore Osun State

Beyond our walls, Osun State holds some of Nigeria's most breathtaking natural and cultural treasures. Let us take you there — our car rental service is available to all guests.

Olumirin Waterfall

Erin-Ijesha · Approx. 45 min drive

A stunning seven-level waterfall nestled in the heart of Erin-Ijesha. Climb the natural rock steps, feel the cool mist, and take in one of Nigeria's most spectacular landscapes — a must-see experience for every guest.

Learn More →

Osun Sacred Grove

Osogbo · UNESCO World Heritage Site

One of the last remaining sacred groves in Yorubaland and a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 2005. A serene forest sanctuary along the Osun River, adorned with shrines, sculptures, and artworks honouring the Yoruba goddess Osun — a profoundly spiritual and cultural experience.

UNESCO Profile →
